Health Tips

Science-backed strategies for better performance and results

🥗 Nutrition
Protein First Rule
Eat protein first in every meal. It reduces blood sugar spikes and keeps you full longer. Target: 0.8g protein per kg of body weight daily.
💧 Hydration
Water Before Meals
Drink 500ml of water 30 minutes before each meal. This boosts metabolism by up to 30% and reduces overeating significantly.
💪 Training
Progressive Overload
Increase weight or reps every week. Without progressive overload, muscle growth plateaus. Consistency beats intensity every time.
😴 Sleep
Sleep = Gains
Without 7-9 hours of sleep, cortisol spikes and muscles break down. Actual muscle growth happens during sleep — don't skip it.
⏰ Timing
Post-Workout Nutrition
Eat protein + carbs within 45 minutes after training. This is the peak window for muscle protein synthesis and recovery.
🧠 Mindset
The 80/20 Rule
80% clean eating, 20% flexibility. A 100% strict diet is not sustainable long-term. Consistency over perfection always wins in fitness.
🇮🇳 Indian Diet
Indian Superfoods
Dal, Paneer, Curd, Eggs, Banana, Oats, Brown Rice, Moong — all budget-friendly and nutrient-rich foods for lean gains and fat loss.
🔥 Fat Loss
Smart Calorie Deficit
Keep your deficit at max 500 kcal. A bigger deficit causes muscle loss and slows metabolism. Slow, steady fat loss = permanent results.
📈 Weight Gain
Clean Bulk Strategy
Only 300–500 kcal surplus. More causes fat gain, not muscle. Target 2g protein per kg body weight. Rice, Banana, Peanut Butter are your friends.
