Health Tips

Science-backed tips for better health

🥗 Nutrition
Protein First Rule
Eat protein first in every meal. It helps reduce blood sugar spikes and keeps you full for longer. Target: body weight ❌ 0.8 g protein per day.
💧 Hydration
Water Before Meals
Drink 500 ml of water 30 minutes before a meal. It can boost metabolism by around 30% and help reduce overeating.
💪 Training
Progressive Overload
Increase your weight or reps slightly every week. Otherwise, muscle growth can plateau. Consistency matters more than intensity.
😴 Sleep
Sleep = Gains
If you don,t get 79 hours of sleep, cortisol levels can rise and lead to muscle breakdown. Muscle actually grows during sleep.
⏰ Timing
Post-Workout Nutrition
Eat protein and carbs within 45 minutes after your workout. This is when muscle protein synthesis is at its peak.
🧠 Mindset
80/20 Rule
Follow 80% clean eating and allow 20% flexibility. A strict 100% diet isn't sustainable long term. Consistency wins over perfection.
🇮🇳 Indian Diet
Best Indian Superfoods
Dhal, paneer, curd, eggs, banana, oats, brown rice, and moong are all cheap, nutrient-rich foods. Great for budget-friendly gains.
🔥 Fat Loss
Calorie Deficit Smart Way
Keep your calorie deficit within 500 kcal. A larger deficit can lead to muscle loss and a slower metabolism. Slow and steady fat loss gives more sustainable results.
📈 Weight Gain
Clean Bulk Strategy
Stick to a 300-500 kcal surplus. Overeating leads to fat gain.Aim for about 2 g of protein per kg of body weight.Foods like rice, banana, and peanut butter can help.
